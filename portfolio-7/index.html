"Polar bears" live in the Arctic and are known for their thick white fur and strong bond with their cubs. They rely on sea ice to hunt and survive, and mothers care deeply for their babies , keeping them safe and warm.

"Ariel" is a curious mermaid princess from Disney "The Little Mermaid" (1989). She dreams of living on land and falls in love with Prince Eric after saving him. To be with him, she makes a deal with the sea witch Ursula, King Triton, turns her into a human so she can live happily with Eric.
